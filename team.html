Substance is being developed by a bunch of volunteers who got excited about the idea. They started contributing one day and haven't stopped.

Michael Aufreiter

Michael is obsessed with the idea of open digital publishing by considering content as data and separating it from presentation. Back in 2010 he kicked off Substance in order to verify that idea. This verification is still in progress.
